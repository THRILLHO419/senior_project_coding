Canada

Although my project focuses on American cities, I wanted to also explore local music from an international perspective. I was particularly interested in Canada, which has many similarities to the United States, but also significant differences in terms of culture, geography, and the role the government plays in arts support. Before YouthSpin each Friday, KOOP airs This Great White North, a show that plays only Canadian music. Doug, an ex-Canadian who founded and co-hosts the show, seemed like the perfect person to discuss what makes Canadaâ€™s music scene distinctive, so I sat down with him and recorded an interview. This originally aired on February 9, 2018.
