Atlanta

After doing two pieces in Austin, I decided to make a piece about some news related to music in Atlanta and Georgia as a whole. I focused my analysis on two pieces of legislation. One was a failed local city council proposal in Atlanta that set limits on where studios could build and another was a successful state bill in Georgia that gave tax credits to music projects in the state to stimulate its music industry. I discussed how these bills ended up failing and passing, respectively, how Georgia’s recent success in the film industry inspired the state law, and how Atlanta’s cultural success informs the bill. This originally aired on December 1, 2017.
